Our Services

Trusted For Our Services

At Dear Logistics, trust isn’t just earned it’s delivered. With every parcel, we bring speed, safety, and sincerity right to your doorstep. From last-mile delivery to cross-country shipping, we handle every move with precision and heart. Our clients trust us because we don’t just deliver goods we deliver promises, on time, every time.

  • 01Truck Load

    FTL means the whole truck is booked by one customer to move a large shipment. It’s faster and safer since the goods go directly from pickup to drop without any stops or mixing with other shipments.

  • 02Partial Load

    LTL is used when the cargo doesn’t fill a full truck. So, goods from different customers are combined in one truck. This saves cost and space, but the delivery may take slightly longer due to multiple stops.

  • 03Last Mile

    This service covers the final step from a warehouse to the customer’s doorstep. It’s super important for e-commerce and retail, and uses smaller vehicles for quick, on-time deliveries.

  • 04 Heavy Cargo

    ODC involves moving oversized or heavyweight items like machinery. It needs special trucks, route planning, and permits. Only expert logistics companies handle this safely and legally.

  • 05 Door Delivery

    In this service, the goods are picked up directly from the sender and delivered straight to the receiver. It’s super convenient and avoids the hassle of dropping or collecting goods at warehouses.

  • 06 Fleet Service

    This gives clients their own set of trucks on contract. It’s perfect for regular deliveries, offering full control over routes, timing, and even truck branding.
